# Design System Documentation: The Machine Spirit Protocol

## 1. Overview & Creative North Star

### Creative North Star: "Tactical Gothicism"
This design system is not a mere interface; it is a battle-hardened HUD forged from the fusion of ancient cathedral aesthetics and brutalist future warfare. It moves away from the "airy" minimalism of modern SaaS and leans into the weight, density, and urgency of a tactical command center. 

The experience must feel **heavy, armored, and intentional.** We achieve this through a "Monolithic Layout" strategy—using hard-edged geometry, asymmetrical data clusters, and high-contrast glowing elements set against a crushing, dark void. We reject the standard grid in favor of "Tactical Nesting," where information is clustered into reinforced modules that mimic the internal displays of a ceramite power-armor helmet.

---

## 2. Colors & Texture

The palette is anchored in the "Blackness of the Void," utilizing deep charcoals and metallic blacks to provide a canvas for high-energy amber and electric blue data-streams.

### The "No-Line" Rule
Standard 1px borders are strictly prohibited for defining sections. In this system, boundaries are created through:
1.  **Tonal Shifts:** A `surface-container-high` (#2a2a2a) module sitting atop a `surface` (#131313) background.
2.  **Texture Junctions:** Transitioning from a brushed metal texture to a carbon-fiber weave defines the edge.
3.  **Light-Bleed:** Using a subtle `secondary` (#9ccaff) glow to define the top edge of a container rather than a solid line.

### Surface Hierarchy & Nesting
Treat the UI as a series of interlocking armored plates.
*   **Base Layer:** `surface` (#131313) - The absolute background.
*   **Secondary Panels:** `surface-container-low` (#1c1b1b) - Large layout sections.
*   **Tactical Modules:** `surface-container-high` (#2a2a2a) - The primary containers for data.
*   **Active Overlays:** `surface-container-highest` (#353534) - Elements that require immediate focus.

### The "Glass & Gradient" Rule
To mimic a HUD glass refraction:
*   Use **Glassmorphism** for floating tactical overlays. Apply `surface_container` colors at 60-80% opacity with a `20px` backdrop blur. 
*   **Signature Gradients:** Buttons and critical alerts should use a linear gradient from `primary` (#ffb87b) to `primary_container` (#ff8f00) at a 45-degree angle to simulate the glow of a physical amber filament.

---

## 3. Typography

The typography scale is designed to balance "Industrial Utility" with "High-Tech Readability."

*   **Display & Headlines (Space Grotesk):** These are your "HUD Readouts." Use `display-lg` for mission-critical titles. The geometric, slightly quirky nature of Space Grotesk provides the "sci-fi" soul. Use `all-caps` for `headline-sm` to create a sense of military authority.
*   **Body & Titles (Inter):** Inter provides the "Technical Manual" feel. It is rugged, legible, and stays out of the way of the tactical data.
*   **Labels (Space Grotesk):** Small-scale data points (e.g., ammo counts, coordinates) must use `label-md` in `primary` (#ffb87b) to pop against the dark background.

---

## 4. Elevation & Depth

In a grimdark interface, depth is not achieved with soft shadows, but with **Tonal Layering** and physical light simulation.

*   **The Layering Principle:** Stack `surface-container` tiers to create "lift." A `surface-container-lowest` card recessed into a `surface-container-high` panel creates a "slotted" effect, common in heavy machinery.
*   **Ambient Shadows:** Use shadows sparingly. When required, use a large, 40px blur with `8%` opacity, tinted with `secondary` (#9ccaff) to simulate the blue light of the screen casting onto the metal armor beneath.
*   **The "Ghost Border" Fallback:** For complex data tables where separation is mandatory, use the `outline-variant` (#564334) at **15% opacity**. This creates a "etched" look rather than a drawn line.
*   **Hard Edges:** All corners are `0px`. Roundness suggests safety; sharpness suggests a weapon.

---

## 5. Components

### Buttons
*   **Primary:** Solid `primary` (#ffb87b) background with `on_primary` (#4c2700) text. No rounded corners. Add a `2px` bottom-only highlight in `primary_fixed_dim` to simulate a physical mechanical key.
*   **Secondary:** Ghost style. `outline` (#a48c7a) "Ghost Border" with `secondary` (#9ccaff) text. On hover, fill with `on_secondary_container` at 20% opacity.

### Tactical Chips
*   Used for status effects (e.g., "Shields Active"). Use `secondary_container` (#1596ef) with a subtle `10%` noise texture overlay to mimic an electronic signal.

### Input Fields
*   Inputs should look like recessed terminals. Use `surface_container_lowest` (#0e0e0e) for the background. The active state should not use a border, but a `primary` (#ffb87b) "glow" line (2px height) at the very bottom of the field.

### Lists & Tables
*   **Forbid Dividers.** Separate list items by alternating between `surface_container_low` and `surface_container_highest`. 
*   **Header:** Use `label-sm` in `tertiary` (#bfc8cc) with a subtle "brushed metal" background texture.

### Additional Component: The "Targeting Reticle" (Iconography)
*   Icons must be encased in bracket-style frames (e.g., `[ ⊕ ]`). 
*   Use silhouettes of futuristic weaponry or gothic gears. 
*   Always apply a subtle `0 0 8px` outer glow to icons using their respective color token (Amber or Blue).

---

## 6. Do's and Don'ts

### Do:
*   **Embrace Asymmetry:** Place heavy data modules on the left and lighter, floating "sensor" data on the right.
*   **Use Texture:** Apply a subtle "Carbon Fiber" pattern to `surface-container-lowest` and a "Brushed Steel" texture to `surface-container-high`.
*   **Flicker Effects:** (In motion) Use a 1% probability opacity flicker on `primary` elements to simulate an aging Machine Spirit.

### Don't:
*   **Don't use Rounded Corners:** No exceptions. The universe is harsh; the UI should be too.
*   **Don't use Pure White:** Use `on_surface` (#e5e2e1) for text. Pure white (#ffffff) is too "clean" and breaks the grimdark immersion.
*   **Don't use Standard Shadows:** Avoid the "floating card" look of Material Design. Elements should feel bolted to the frame.

### Accessibility Note:
While the theme is dark, ensure all `primary` text on `surface` backgrounds maintains a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1. The amber glow (#ffb87b) is specifically chosen to remain legible against the charcoal void.```
